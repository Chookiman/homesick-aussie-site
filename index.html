What We're About

HomesickAussie isn't another travel blog. We're the platform where expat Aussies share the real stuff - the food disappointments, the cultural shock moments, and those rare discoveries that make living abroad a bit more bearable.

Every story is written by someone who's been there. Someone who understands that you can love your new country and still miss the way things taste at home.

We're not here to sell you anything. We're here to share what works, call out what doesn't, and help fellow Aussies navigate the weird world of international comfort food.

Because someone's gotta find the good stuff so you don't have to suffer.
